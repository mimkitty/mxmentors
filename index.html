Mentors Denelle Pantleo and Stacey Back have a combined 20 years of experiencein the massage therapy field, as well as 8 years in massage therapy education and administration. Their passion for expanding the reach and impact of massage therapy has led them to this new partnership venture. The goal of Massage Mentors is to make quality continuing education and professional advisement more widely available and affordable for licensed massage therapists of Southwest Missouri.

MISSION

Offering continuing education and MBLEx preparatory tutoring for massage therapists in Springfield Missouri and surrounding areas.
